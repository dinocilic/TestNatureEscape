Hiking

Hiking in Bosnia and Herzegovina is a very attractive activity cause here you can find many mountains to climb. Almost every city in Bosnia has a Mountain centers where you can get help and information about your trip.

Also if you need information on certain mountain or route to go, you can contact us and we can help you.

Lake Exploring

It's a known fact that Bosnia and Herzegovina is one of the richest countries with water in Europe, and with breathtaking lakes and rivers.

Natural lakes in B&H cover around 65.1 square kilometers, if we compare this surface with the entire surface of B&H, then natural lakes cover 0,11% of it's total surface.

There is a really long list of lakes which embellish this land but we have picked the most famouse ones for you.

Stećak tombstones

Stečak tombstones are medieval monolithic stone monuments associated with the entire area of today's Bosnia and Herzegovina.

There are over 60,000 tombstones in B&H, but we will mention you the most interesing ones and most visited, and they are Dugo Polje on Blidinje (Jablanica), Grčka glavica near Konjic, Boljuni in Stolac, Ravanjska Vrata on Kupres, Radimlja near Stolac.
